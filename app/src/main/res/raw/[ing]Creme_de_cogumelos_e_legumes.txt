Ingredientes:
potatoes
400g
Onions
2
garlic cloves
2 
water
1.2liters
canned mushrooms
500g
fresh mushrooms 
100g
olive oil
1 spuns of soup
q.d
salt
Preparação: 
1. Peel potatoes, onions and garlic and cut into pieces, as well as leek. Put everything in a pan with water and canned mushrooms and season with salt. Bring to the boil for 25 minutes.
2. Cut the fresh mushrooms in quarters and sauté them in olive oil. Remove them from the heat and set aside. Grind the cooked vegetables and bring the soup back to the heat with the cream. Bring to a boil and remove. Serve with sautéed mushrooms.
